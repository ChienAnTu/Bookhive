Payment Gateway (e.g., Stripe).


1.     Security Deposit Handling

When a borrower requests a book, the platform collects a security deposit and shipping fee through the payment gateway.

The deposit is held by the platform until the book is returned.

If the book is returned on time and in good condition, the deposit is refunded to the borrower.

If there is a violation (late return, damage, loss) or returning overtime, part or all of the deposit is paid to the lender as compensation.


2.     Purchase & Service Fee

If the book is purchased, the borrower pays through the payment gateway.

The platform automatically deducts a service fee (through set the percentage) before sending the remaining amount to the lender.


3.     Shipping Fee

The borrower pays an actual shipping fee up front which is updated by borrower.

The shipping fee is used for delivery or passed to the lender after shipping is confirmed.


4.     Payment Status and Transparency

All payment status updates (deposit held, refunded, paid to lender, service fee deducted) are shown to both borrower and lender in real time.


5.     Dispute & Manual Handling

If there is a dispute or error, admins can manually release or refund deposits.


6.     Donation support

Donation support and fee configuration panel


7.     Audit & Security

All payment actions are logged.

No sensitive card data is stored on the platform.
